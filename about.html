    Who We Are

    JK Project Engineers is headed by our visionary leader Mr R B Prasad, an Engineer from NIT fabrication and erection industry for close to 40 years and is the Managing Director of Prasad & Co., a well-known and established firm. The proprietor of JK Project Engineers, Mr Kunal Prasad, has a Masters degree in Finance from University College Dublin, Ireland. He is a vivid entrepreneur and handles the business under the guidance of Mr Prasad.
    Throughout his amazing journey from establishing Prasad & Co. in 1980 with around 5 employees to building a steel fabrication empire of this magnitude is the proof of his dedication and hard-work, which is why JK Project Engineers is proud to be headed by him and definitely looks forward to achieving great heights. Quality first is our focus to deliver highest with and deliver complete Customer Satisfaction.
    Established on 12th February 2016, JKPE is a highly diversified, multidisciplinary contractor & one of the best tank fabricator in the industry. Our team has earned its place in today’s competitive market through our combination of core believes and superior services and quality end products. The individualized care and unwavering attention that we bring to all our projects are deciding factors in why our clients always choose us. As a pioneering steel fabrication unit, the emphasis is always on the achievements of four indispensable goals.
    JKPEs crew of skilled and experienced professionals combined with our unparalleled dedication and complete client focus have been sure a way to make every project a success.
